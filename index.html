The problem

If you work solo, your business often ends up spread across chats, notes, inboxes, and too many tools.

  • missed follow-ups
  • unclear priorities
  • offers without a visible pipeline
  • content handled inconsistently
  • too much operational noise

The solution

Solo Business OS gives you a simple structure to run the core parts of a one-person business:

  • dashboard overview
  • lead tracker
  • offer pipeline
  • weekly planner
  • content tracker
  • mini SOP library

Who it is for

  • freelancers
  • consultants
  • solo founders
  • small digital operators

Who it is not for

  • larger teams
  • enterprise CRM needs
  • complex multi-user businesses
