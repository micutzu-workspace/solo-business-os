# Pre-Launch Review — Solo Business OS

## Goal
Catch the main legal, financial, and operational risks before Cristi creates the product listing.

## Claims review
Safe current claims:
- organizes leads, offers, tasks, and weekly execution in one system
- built for freelancers and solo founders
- simple setup and lightweight operating workflow

Avoid or soften unless proven:
- guaranteed revenue growth
- guaranteed productivity improvement
- claims that imply business results are certain

## Compliance checks before listing goes live
- product description matches what is actually included
- screenshots and examples do not expose client or personal data
- any template terms or license text are clear
- refund position is stated clearly on the sales platform
- payout and tax details match the legal account holder

## Operational checks
- final deliverable folder is clean and easy to download
- quick-start guide matches the actual template structure
- sample data is generic and safe to share
- setup checklist can be completed in around 20 minutes by a new buyer

## Recommendation
Next best step is to package the deliverable and add a short license/usage note so the product is easier to publish safely.

## Blocker
Live listing setup still requires Cristi approval and direct access because it touches sales platform account creation/configuration.
