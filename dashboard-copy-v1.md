# Dashboard Copy — Solo Business OS v1

## Welcome block
Welcome to Solo Business OS.

This system is designed to help you run a one-person business with less chaos and more control.

Use this dashboard as your main operating page. The goal is not to track everything. The goal is to keep the most important moving parts visible and manageable.

## Suggested dashboard sections
### Weekly focus
Your top outcomes for the current week.

### Active leads
The opportunities that still need attention.

### Open offers
The proposals or quotes currently in motion.

### This week tasks
The tasks that matter now, not everything you could do.

### Content in progress
Ideas and drafts that are actively moving toward publication.

### SOP quick links
Short operating routines for the recurring parts of the business.

## Short usage note
Check this page daily. Keep it useful, not perfect.
