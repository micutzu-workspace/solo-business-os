# Setup Checklist Insert Copy — Solo Business OS v1

## Title
Setup Checklist

## Checklist
- Duplicate the template into your own Notion workspace
- Rename the dashboard to match your business
- Add 3–10 active leads
- Add any current offers or proposals
- Set your top 3 priorities for the week
- Add your current tasks
- Add 3–5 content ideas
- Review the SOP pages
- Archive or delete sample entries you do not need
- Use the system for 7 days before major customization
