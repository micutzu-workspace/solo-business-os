# SOP Pack — Solo Business OS

## SOP 1 — Capture a lead
### Objective
Make sure no real opportunity disappears into chat history or notes.

### Steps
1. Add the lead immediately to Lead Tracker
2. Record source, need, and next action
3. Assign a follow-up date
4. Add any relevant notes

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: vague next step
  - Mitigation: use a specific action and date

---

## SOP 2 — Qualify quickly
### Objective
Avoid wasting time on weak or mismatched leads.

### Steps
1. Identify problem urgency
2. Check buying intent
3. Estimate value range
4. Decide: continue, nurture, or archive

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: keeping dead leads too long
  - Mitigation: archive when no clear next step exists

---

## SOP 3 — Follow up consistently
### Objective
Increase response rate without becoming spammy.

### Steps
1. Review due follow-ups daily
2. Send one clear and polite follow-up
3. Log the outcome
4. Set next date or close the loop

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: excessive follow-up
  - Mitigation: keep cadence reasonable and respectful

---

## SOP 4 — Send an offer
### Objective
Make offers traceable and easier to close.

### Steps
1. Add the prospect to Offer Pipeline
2. Record offer type and quoted value
3. Mark sent date
4. Set next step
5. Track final result

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: sending offers without a follow-up plan
  - Mitigation: every offer gets a next step

---

## SOP 5 — Plan the week
### Objective
Avoid reactive work and keep the business moving.

### Steps
1. Define top 3 outcomes
2. Separate sales, delivery, and admin tasks
3. Move non-essential tasks to backlog
4. Review daily

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: too many priorities
  - Mitigation: limit active weekly outcomes to three

---

## SOP 6 — Track content simply
### Objective
Maintain consistent visibility without content chaos.

### Steps
1. Capture ideas in one place
2. Assign format and channel
3. Set status and CTA
4. Reuse good ideas in multiple formats

### Risks + mitigations
- Risk: content without business purpose
  - Mitigation: each item should have a clear CTA or role
