# Distribution Plan — Solo Business OS

## Goal
Get the first sales with low cost, low complexity, and minimal contractual involvement.

## Initial channels

1. X / Twitter posts from personal account
2. LinkedIn posts from personal account
3. Relevant communities where promotion is allowed
4. Direct sharing to interested contacts only after approval
5. Product listing on Payhip or Gumroad

## Messaging angles

- "I built a simple system for solo operators who are tired of scattered tools."
- "A practical Notion operating system for freelancers and solo founders."
- "If your leads, tasks, and offers live in five places, this fixes that."

## Launch approach

### Phase 1
- Build MVP
- Prepare listing
- Publish product page
- Post 2–3 organic launch messages

### Phase 2
- Gather objections and feedback
- Improve headline, screenshots, and offer
- Add one bonus if needed

### Phase 3
- Turn feedback into v2 or adjacent product

## Constraints
- No spam
- No fake urgency
- No misleading income claims
- Respect platform rules and disclosure norms
