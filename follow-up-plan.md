# Follow-up Plan — Solo Business OS

## Goal
Turn early signs of interest into useful validation without overcommitting or creating legal/commercial noise too early.

## Stage 1 — early access signal
- publish landing page
- share 2–3 posts
- collect inbound interest by email

## Stage 2 — classify responses manually
Look for:
- who is responding
- what problem they mention
- whether they would plausibly buy
- repeated wording patterns

## Stage 3 — validate positioning
Questions to answer from responses:
- Do people understand the product quickly?
- Which pain point resonates most?
- Is the product perceived as useful enough to try or buy?

## Stage 4 — next action after signal
If signal is weak:
- change copy
- tighten audience
- simplify promise

If signal is decent:
- build the first shippable version
- prepare a simple paid release path
- tighten privacy and commercial docs before payment flow

## Guardrails
- no custom promises to early users
- no misleading claims
- no collecting unnecessary personal data
- no aggressive follow-up cadence
