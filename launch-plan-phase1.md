# Launch Plan — Phase 1 (Autonomy-First)

## Objective
Validate demand for Solo Business OS without depending on a fragile marketplace flow.

## Phase 1 assets
- landing page
- early access form
- clear positioning
- launch posts
- follow-up email draft

## Execution sequence
1. Publish landing page
2. Connect waitlist form
3. Publish 2–3 launch posts
4. Collect signups and signals
5. Review objections and patterns
6. Decide on checkout stack only after first validation

## Success signals
- waitlist signups
- replies or questions from target users
- repeated pain points
- evidence that the positioning resonates

## Risk controls
- no misleading claims
- no premature checkout complexity
- no unnecessary spending before signal
- no custom promises beyond the MVP scope
