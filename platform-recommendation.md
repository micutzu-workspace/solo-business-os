# Platform Recommendation — Solo Business OS

## Goal
Choose the simplest compliant path to sell a digital product with minimal setup and low operational risk.

## Recommended order

### 1. Payhip
Why:
- easy digital product setup
- supports simple product pages
- low friction for MVP launches
- suitable for lightweight selling

### 2. Gumroad
Why:
- well-known for digital products
- fast setup
- simple checkout flow

### 3. Lemon Squeezy
Why:
- cleaner long-term commercial setup
- more structure for scaling
- better later than day-one MVP in this case

## Caution
Before going live, check:
- account identity matches payout setup
- tax and payout details are accurate
- refund policy is clear
- product description makes no misleading claims
- platform terms allow your intended product and marketing style

## Current recommendation
Use Payhip first unless a platform-specific issue appears during setup.
