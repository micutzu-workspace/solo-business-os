# Post-Signal Review Framework — Solo Business OS

## Goal
Turn inbound interest into better product decisions.

## Questions to answer after first responses
1. Do people understand the product quickly?
2. Which problem resonates most?
3. Is the audience more freelancer, consultant, or solo founder?
4. Is the current promise too broad?
5. Are people interested in seeing it, or in buying it?

## Output format
- volume of responses
- number of strong / medium / weak signals
- top repeated pain points
- confusing wording found in responses
- recommended copy change
- recommended product scope change

## Decision rule
- If strong signals are scarce, narrow the audience and sharpen the problem.
- If strong signals are real, move toward the first shippable product version.
