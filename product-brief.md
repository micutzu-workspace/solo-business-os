# Product Brief — Solo Business OS

## 1. Objective
Create a low-risk digital asset that can be launched quickly, sold repeatedly, and does not depend on custom client contracts.

## 2. Concrete steps
- Build a simple but useful system in Notion format
- Package it with quick-start documentation and SOPs
- Sell via a lightweight checkout platform
- Validate with a low-friction early price
- Expand into adjacent assets after first sales

## 3. Estimated cost
0–50 EUR for MVP stage

## 4. Time to first results
7–14 days for first launchable version

## 5. Risks + mitigations
- Risk: too generic
  - Mitigation: focus on solo service operators, not everyone
- Risk: weak perceived value
  - Mitigation: sell clarity and operating leverage, not just a template
- Risk: low demand signal
  - Mitigation: keep MVP small and test positioning early

## 6. Revenue estimate
- Conservative: 50–150 EUR/month
- Realistic: 300–1,000 EUR/month
- Optimistic: 2,000+ EUR/month

## 7. Approval needed now
None for internal drafting and product design
