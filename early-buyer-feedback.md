# Early Buyer Feedback Prompt — Solo Business OS

## Short version
What felt most useful, what felt unclear, and what almost stopped you from using this template?

## Slightly more structured version
1. What was the most useful part?
2. What felt confusing or unnecessary?
3. Was setup actually doable in about 20 minutes?
4. What would make this feel more valuable?
5. Would you recommend it to another solo operator? Why or why not?

## Goal
Use early feedback to improve positioning, onboarding, and perceived value before expanding the product.
