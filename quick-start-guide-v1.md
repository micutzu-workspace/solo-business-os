# Quick-Start Guide — Solo Business OS v1

## What this is
Solo Business OS is a practical operating system for freelancers and solo founders who want to run a one-person business with less chaos and more control.

## What to do first
1. Duplicate the template into your own Notion workspace
2. Rename the dashboard to match your business
3. Add your active leads
4. Add any current offers
5. Set your weekly priorities
6. Add current content ideas
7. Review the SOP pages once

## Best way to use it
- check the dashboard daily
- review follow-ups consistently
- keep weekly priorities short and realistic
- use the same system for at least 7 days before customizing it heavily

## What this product is not
- not a full enterprise CRM
- not a complex automation suite
- not a team operating system

## What it is meant to do well
- reduce operational noise
- keep key business items visible
- create a simple operating rhythm
